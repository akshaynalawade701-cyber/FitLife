Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This site stores your workout plans and nutrition logs locally on your device using localStorage. We do not transmit this data to any server.

Data stored

  • Theme preference
  • Saved workout plans
  • Nutrition goals and meals

Data control

You can clear your browser storage or use the reset options in the app to remove your data. You can also export your data as JSON.

For questions, contact us via the form on the homepage.
